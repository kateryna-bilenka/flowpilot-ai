# FlowPilot AI

## Product Vision

**Version:** 0.1

**Status:** Approved

**Author:** Kateryna Bilenka

**Last Updated:** 30 June 2026

---

## Vision

Empower Project Managers to focus on leadership instead of administration by providing an AI-powered workspace that transforms project ideas into structured execution plans within minutes.

---

## Mission

FlowPilot AI helps project managers start projects faster by automatically generating essential project documentation, planning artifacts and collaboration assets from a single project idea.

---

## Why We Exist

Project Managers spend too much time creating documentation, organizing information and switching between disconnected tools.

FlowPilot AI removes repetitive work while keeping the Project Manager in control of every decision.

---

## Long-term Vision

Become the AI workspace where every new project begins.

Instead of opening multiple tools before starting a project, teams open FlowPilot AI.

One idea.

One workspace.

One intelligent kickoff.
